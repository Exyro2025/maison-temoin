The costliest defects are not the dramatic ones — they’re the quiet deviations that pass unnoticed during build and surface after handoff. Maison Témoin provides an internal authority layer for residential developers: structured field observations, manufacturer-standard verification, and a serialized ledger of system behavior — established before keys are in hand. — Standardized stewardship framework applied consistently nationwide.

Maison Témoin observes and documents only. No repairs are performed. All corrective action remains the domain of licensed trades and the developer’s existing process.

The Maison Témoin Framework

A system built for continuity, clarity, and defensible documentation — not generic “property management.”

Indice Jour Un™ — Baseline Index

A descriptive baseline established at the first engagement. Indice Jour Un™ is reference-only — not a score, rating, certification, code-compliance signoff, or warranty determination.

Registre Maison™ — The Project Ledger

A serialized record of each engagement with timestamped photos, observations, and triage notes. Maintained as a continuity file for the developer and transfers with the asset when applicable.

Audit Maison Garde™

The structured observational protocol. Systems behavior, moisture risk points, life-safety items, and envelope conditions are logged to the Registre Maison™.

Couche Prévention™ — Prevention Layer

Loss-prevention actions (e.g., condensate verification, moisture checks, water-system health actions, dryer-vent condition) documented for continuity and routed when trade verification is required.

Bouclier Grille™ — Grid Shield

Electrical and load observations, including thermal panel scanning and grid-stress diagnostics, to assess EV and battery readiness amid rising demand.

Structured Triage

Every observation is categorized: Education • Routine Maintenance • Warranty-Potential • Urgent Safety. All routing remains centralized through Maison Témoin.

Boundaries

Designed for legal clarity and clean responsibility lines between developer, homeowner, and licensed trades.

Not a Score, Certification, or Signoff

Indice Jour Un™ is a descriptive baseline reference only. It is not a rating, grade, certification, code-compliance approval, engineering signoff, or warranty determination.

No Repairs Performed

Maison Témoin operates as an independent observational authority. We provide defensible documentation for the Registre Maison™; all corrective actions remain the domain of licensed trades and the developer’s existing process.

Not Warranty Administration

Warranty-Potential items are routed to the developer’s established workflow. Developer obligations remain unchanged.

Single Coordination Channel

All requests and next steps flow through Maison Témoin. Field observers do not quote, diagnose, or schedule work independently.
