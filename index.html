The Maison Témoin Framework: Residential Risk Intelligence

A disciplined architecture of baseline science, serialized continuity, and predictive routing — engineered for high-value residential portfolios.

Indice Jour Un™ | The Baseline Index

An uncompromising technical baseline established at the point of first engagement. Indice Jour Un™ serves as the definitive reference point — monitoring for deviations from engineered performance standards rather than simple code compliance or binary pass/fail ratings.

Registre Maison™ | The Serialized Ledger

A persistent digital continuity file capturing the “medical history” of the asset. Every observation, thermal scan, and technical triage is serialized — providing developers with a defensive data trail and homeowners with a record of stewardship that transfers with the property.

Audit Maison Garde™ | Structured Protocol

Our exhaustive observational protocol. We analyze system behavior, moisture dynamics, and envelope integrity. Each data point is cross-referenced against Manufacturer Golden Standards to identify quiet deviations before they become dramatic failures.

Couche Prévention™ | The Prevention Layer

A dedicated loss-prevention layer focused on high-risk mechanical junctions. From condensate path verification to appliance coil health and dryer-vent backpressure, findings are documented and routed for licensed trade verification prior to closeout.

Bouclier Grille™ | Grid Shield

Advanced electrical and grid-stress diagnostics. Utilizing thermal panel scanning and load analysis, we assess readiness for high-demand loads including EV infrastructure and battery storage — ensuring the electrical “heart” of the home is resilient.

Structured Triage | Predictive Routing

Risk is not a monolith. Every observation is filtered through structured triage and categorized from Routine Maintenance to Systemic Risk. This routing allows developers to manage warranty exposure with scientific precision.

Maison Témoin: Strategic Boundaries

Designed for legal clarity and clean responsibility lines between the Developer, Homeowner, and Licensed Trades.

The Independent Observational Authority

Maison Témoin functions strictly as an observational documentation layer. Indice Jour Un™ is a descriptive baseline reference only — not a rating, grade, certification, code-compliance approval, engineering signoff, or warranty determination.

No Repairs Performed

Maison Témoin does not execute corrective work. We provide structured documentation for the Registre Maison™; all corrective actions remain the exclusive domain of licensed trades and the developer’s existing process.

Not Warranty Administration

Warranty-potential findings are routed directly into the developer’s established workflow. Developer obligations and consumer warranty terms remain unchanged.

Single Coordination Channel

All requests and next steps flow through Maison Témoin to preserve project integrity. Field observers do not quote, diagnose, or schedule work independently.

The Developer Value: Risk Insulation
These boundaries allow Maison Témoin to deliver a defensive documentation layer without assuming the liabilities of a trade contractor or a warrantor. Responsibility remains precisely where it belongs: with licensed experts and the developer’s chain of command.
