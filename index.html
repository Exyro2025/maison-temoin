Post-Close Stewardship

The first 12 months after closing are where complexity meets expectation. Maison Témoin provides a structured stewardship layer included at closing: four scheduled visits, triage discipline, and a serialized record of care — preserving the Indice Jour Un™ across private estates and new developments through a standardized stewardship framework applied consistently nationwide.

Maison Témoin observes and documents only. Corrective work is performed by licensed trades.

The Maison Témoin Framework

A system built for continuity, clarity, and defensible documentation — not generic “property management.”

Indice Jour Un™ — Day-One Index

A descriptive baseline established at the first visit. The Indice Jour Un is a reference state only — not a score, rating, certification, or warranty determination.

Registre Maison™ — The Home Ledger

A serialized digital record of each visit, with timestamped photos, observations, and triage notes. Transfers with the home as proof of stewardship.

Audit Maison Garde™

The quarterly observational protocol. Systems behavior, moisture risk points, life-safety items, and exterior envelope conditions are logged to the Registre Maison.

Couche Prévention™ — Prevention Layer

Loss-prevention actions (e.g., condensate verification, moisture checks, water-system health actions, dryer-vent condition).

Bouclier Grille™ — Grid Shield

High-fidelity electrical and load observations, including thermal panel scanning and grid-stress diagnostics, to assess EV and battery readiness amid rising demand.

Structured Triage

Every observation is categorized: Education • Routine Maintenance • Warranty-Potential • Urgent Safety. All coordination flows through Maison Témoin.
